MensaProtocol
MensaProtocol is a across-chain automatic token swap protocol. It is designed to be both easier to use and safer. Instead of an order book system, it will use the AMM model, with prices are automatically adjusted according to what people buy and sell in the exchange. In addition, MensaProtocol is optimized on the basis of traditional AMM algorithm, which will reduce the volatile losses of market makers, which is very friendly to liquidity providers. Meanwhile, MensaProtocol will also have lower gas rates, making trading more favorable.
MensaEarn
MensaEarn will automatically divide the funds into higher earning trading contracts. You can regard this as a decentralized quantitative trading system. Moreover, the rewards from MensaEarn will include but not limited to COMP, SYN, etc., and all these benefits will be used to buy back the MensaToken.
Proceeds from transaction fees will be used periodically to buy back and destroy tokens and meanwhile, the proceeds of our MensaEarn will be used for repurchase. MensaToken will be the only token of value in circulation in our protocol, which will bring out constant deflation.
In addition to gains in the secondary market, token also serves as a governance token in the Mensa Protocol and can be used as a vote for the whole ecology. Holding tokens also allows you to receive ecological project airdrops from time to time.
The supply and price of the token are entirely determined by the community, with no caps or restrictions. At the same time, the FOMO financing incentive model is set. The team will only get 8% of total token based on the amount of token produced and conduct a 5-year linear lock. And in honor of Hayden Adams and Vitalik Buterin, we will share 0.5 and 1 percent of our 8 percent tokens with them, respectively.
