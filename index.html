About
Hayden Adams first launched Uniswap, a fluid-based system, in 2018. The initial version was a bit limited and simple, but was still good compared to EtherDelta UX. As time goes on, Uniswap has more updates, such as ring matching, but still limited in a single chain, with- out better extension, makes the value of different filling can not be better reversed. As a result, we upgrade UniswapX on the basis of cross chains. At the beginning we wanted to use the name of the other, but in order to make people better cognitive products, and salute to Adams, we didn't do too much on the name changes. On product use, we will incorporate with Polkadot and Nervous technology, trans- ferring of more value chain liquidity support.
UniswapX
The Uniswap protocol was developed by a small team in Brooklyn, New York, with partners around the world. It provides good liquidity support for Defi products and is an important application in Defi. Based on Uniswap, we have upgraded and optimised it and partnered with other existing cross-chain infrastructures to provide better mobility services for other projects besides Defi Ecology on Ethereum.
SwapXChain
Based on the current status of blockchain infrastructure, we integrate several recognised excellent cross-chain solutions and the existing situation of anonymity. We will launch our own cross-chain anonymous solution, which will be an optimised and upgraded version based on ZEC's zero-knowledge proof.
It will have the following features:
1. High TPS 10000/s
2. Optimised and upgraded verification mechanism of zero-knowledge proof with high anonymity
3. POS mechanism similar to Ethereum.
