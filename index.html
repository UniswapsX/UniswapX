As time goes on, Uniswap has more updates, such as ring matching, but still limited in a single chain, with- out better extension, makes the value of different filling can not be better reversed. On product use, we will incorporate with Polkadot and Nervous technology, trans- ferring of more value chain liquidity support.
UniswapX
UniswapX is a across-chain automatic token swap protocol. It is designed to be both easier to use and safer. Instead of an order book system, it will use the AMM model, with prices are automatically adjusted according to what people buy and sell in the exchange. In addition, UniswapX is optimized on the basis of traditional AMM algorithm, which will reduce the volatile losses of market makers, which is very friendly to liquidity providers. Meanwhile, UniswapX will also have lower gas rates, making trading more favorable.
